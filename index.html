Warren Buffett

American investor, Business tycoon, and Philanthropist

  • 1930 - Born in Omaha, Nebraska
  • 1947 - Graduated from Woodrow Wilson High School
  • 1949 - At age 19, graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration
    from the University of Nebraska
  • 1951 - He earned a Master of Science in Economics from Columbia.
  • 1951-54 - Buffett-Falk & Co. as an investment salesman
  • 1954-56 - Graham-Newman Corp. as a securities analyst
  • 1956-69 - Buffett Partnership, Ltd. as a general partner
  • 1970- CEO of Berkshire Hathaway Inc.
  • 1977 - Purchased the Buffalo Evening News
  • 1979 - Aquired a 25% share of ABC
  • 1987 - Became chairman of Salomon
  • 1988 - Bought 7% share of Coca-Cola for 1 billion dollars
  • 1990 - He became a billionaire
  • 2014 - His net worth was $64.2 billion
